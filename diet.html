Diet: Panthers are strictly carnivores: they only eat meat.

Predators: American panthers living in the southern part of Florida share marshy territory with alligators, which is why these two dominant predators occasionally encounter each other in the wild.

Sleep Habits: Sleep a lot, Dine rarely. Panthers may only eat once a week and sleep as much as 18 hours a day.
